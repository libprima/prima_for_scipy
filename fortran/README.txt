This is the reference implementation of Powell's derivative-free optimization method COBYLA.

Coded by Zaikun ZHANG (www.zhangzk.net) based on the following paper and Powell's code, with
modernization, bug fixes, and improvements.

M. J. D. Powell, A direct search optimization method that models the objective and constraint
functions by linear interpolation, In Advances in Optimization and Numerical Analysis, eds. S. Gomez
and J. P. Hennart, pages 51--67, Springer Verlag, Dordrecht, Netherlands, 1994

See the directory "original" for Powell's code.

Dedicated to late Professor M. J. D. Powell FRS (1936--2015).

Started in July 2020.
